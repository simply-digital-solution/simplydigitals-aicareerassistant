What is AI Career Assistant?

AI Career Assistant is an intelligent job search platform built for beginners, junior professionals, and mid-level career individuals navigating a competitive job market. It consolidates every step of the job search — discovering roles, evaluating your fit, tailoring your resume, preparing for interviews, and tracking your applications — into a single, streamlined workflow.

Instead of spending hours manually searching job boards, rewriting resumes, and guessing whether you are a good fit, AI Career Assistant handles the repetitive work intelligently, giving you more time to focus on preparation and performance.

How It Works

A complete job search workflow in six clear steps — set up once, run continuously.

1

Manage every application through Research → Selected → Applied → Interview → Offer. Your personal job search CRM.

Why It Matters Now

The job market has become significantly more competitive. Widespread industry restructuring and layoffs mean that roles attract hundreds of applicants. Recruiters spend an average of six to seven seconds reviewing a resume before moving on, and many organisations use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter candidates automatically before a human ever sees the application.

  • ATS systems reject up to 75% of applications before they reach a recruiter
  • Job seekers without optimised resumes are filtered out regardless of their actual capability
  • Manually tailoring resumes and researching each role is time-consuming and unsustainable at scale
  • AI Career Assistant levels the playing field — giving every job seeker the same tools previously available only to those who could afford professional resume services

Know Your Fit Before You Apply

Applying to roles you are significantly underqualified for wastes time and affects your confidence. AI Career Assistant's Role Fit Analysis evaluates your profile against the market and identifies the roles where you are most competitive.

Strong Match Roles

Roles where your skills and experience align well — highest probability of progressing to interview.

Stretch Goals

Roles slightly above your current level — worth pursuing with a strong tailored application.

Adjacent Career Moves

Roles in related areas that leverage your transferable skills — useful for career pivots.

Skill Gap Identification

Clearly shows what is missing so you can prioritise learning and development effectively.

Your AI Assistant, Not Your Replacement

AI Career Assistant is built on a large language model (LLM) — the same technology that powers leading AI tools. However, it is designed with a clear principle: the AI handles repetitive, time-consuming tasks while you retain full control over every decision.

  • The AI scores jobs, tailors resumes and generates interview questions — you decide which jobs to pursue and which applications to send
  • Every generated resume is a starting point — you review, edit and approve before it represents you
  • The tool surfaces insights and recommendations; your judgement and personal narrative remain central
  • Built to empower people entering and progressing in the workforce — not to automate them out of the process
Using AI to find a job is not unfair — it is the same as using a professional resume writer or career coach. AI Career Assistant makes that level of support accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford it.

Secure Storage

All data is stored on secured infrastructure. Your resume text and profile information are accessible only within your private account.

Why Your Resumes Live on Google Drive

When AI Career Assistant generates a tailored resume for you, it is saved directly to your own Google Drive account — not on our servers. This is a deliberate design decision that puts you in full control of your documents.

  • You own your resumes completely — they live in your Google Drive, not ours
  • Access, edit, download and share your resumes directly from Drive at any time, from any device
  • If you disconnect Google Drive or stop using the service, your documents remain yours — nothing is lost
  • Reduces the amount of sensitive personal data held on our infrastructure, consistent with our PDPA obligations
  • Google Drive provides version history, so you can track how your resume has evolved over time
Connecting Google Drive is optional. You can still use all other features of AI Career Assistant without it. Drive integration is only required if you wish to save generated resumes to your personal storage.

It Gets Smarter With You

  • A complete profile produces more relevant job matches and higher-quality tailored resumes

  • The tool grows with you — useful at every stage from your first job search to your next career move
